About Me

Hello! I'm Tony Illenden, a recent graduate from the University of Oklahoma's School of Meteorology, where I earned my Bachelor of Science in Meteorology. I'm a graduate research and teaching assistant at Northern Illinois University, working toward my Master of Science. My research topic is studying a short-term predictability issue of frontal waves in atmospheric rivers. After grad school, I aspire to work at the National Weather Service, where I can provide life-saving forecasts and warnings to the public.
